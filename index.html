The Foundation for Enlightened Society (FES) is a non-profit based in Boulder, CO, supporting the transmission of the practicing lineages of the Tibetan Rimé tradition, especially the Kagyu, Nyingma, and Shambhala lineages of Chögyam Trungpa Rinpoche.

Current Initiatives

Our goal is to ensure that the precious lineages we have received continue robustly into the future.
To that end, we are currently focused on meeting the needs of newer and younger practitioners.

  1. Supporting newer tantrikas and sadhakas by coordinating ngöndro practice, lungs, feasts. We are especially focused on getting ready for the 2024 Vajrayogini Abhisheka in Halifax and offering continuing education and practice opportunities following its conclusion.
  2. We are providing seminary transcripts and other materials to participants in the 3 Yanas Retreat at Drala Mountain Center. If you would like to donate some, see the Donations section below!
  3. Offering programming and resources for newer practitioners of Buddhist and Shambhala paths such as study groups.
  4. Receiving and distributing dharma materials such as texts, liturgies, and ritual objects. If you would like to donate such materials, we will store, care for, and appropriately distribute them to practitioners who may put them to good use.
  5. Providing training in administrative roles within the Buddhist world through volunteering and staffing major events such as Rabjam Rinpoche’s 2023 visit to Boulder.
