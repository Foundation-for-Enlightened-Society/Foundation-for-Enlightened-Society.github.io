FES is a non-profit organization that supports training in meditative view and practice, especially the Tibetan Buddhist Rimé (non-sectarian) and Shambhala traditions taught by Chögyam Trungpa Rinpoche.

We welcome everyone, but we are especially focused on supporting newer students and those with more experience who have drifted away.

Recurring Programs

FES offers several recurring programs in Boulder and online, including a dharma discussion group, dharma teaching practice group, ngöndro practice, Werma Sadhana practice, and social events. Signup for our mailing list to receive notifications about events or contact practice@fes.ngo.

Dharma Materials

FES receives, organizes, and passes on dharma materials, especially from experienced practitioners who do not need them anymore, to newer folks who do. If you'd like to donate or request materials, please contact donations@fes.ngo.

Practice House

We are researching the feasability of establishing a lay residential practice center inspired by Marpa House and KCCL.

Recent Initiatives

  1. We received many boxes of Vajradhatu Seminary transcripts and delievered them to participants in the 3 Yanas Retreat at Drala Mountain Center.
  2. We helped students prepare for the 2024 Vajrayogini Abhisheka in Halifax by arranging reading transmissions, hosting group ngöndro practice, and generally providing fellowship during the challenging work of the preliminaries.
  3. We ran the security and venue team for Rabjam Rinpoche's 2023 visit to Boulder, helping to pass on the tradition of mindfully protecting visiting dignitaries and guests.
