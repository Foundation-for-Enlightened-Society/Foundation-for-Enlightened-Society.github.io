FES is a non-profit organization that supports training in meditation and philosophy, especially the Buddhist and Shambhala traditions as taught by Chögyam Trungpa Rinpoche.

We welcome everyone, but we are especially focused on supporting newer students.

Ongoing Offerings

We offer several recurring programs in Boulder, Colorado and online, including a dharma discussion group, dharma teaching practice group, ngöndro practice, Werma Sadhana practice, social events, etc.

Mailing List

The best way to stay up to date is to subscribe to our mailing list by emailing practice@fes.ngo.

Dharma House

We are researching the feasability of establishing a lay dharma house inspired by Marpa House and KCCL.

Recent Initiatives

  1. We created a program to receive, organize, and pass on dharma materials, especially from experienced practitioners who do not need them anymore, to newer folks who do.
  2. We received many boxes of Vajradhatu Seminary transcripts and delievered them to participants in the 3 Yanas Retreat at Drala Mountain Center.
  3. We helped students qualify for the 2024 Vajrayogini Abhisheka in Halifax by arranging reading transmissions, hosting group ngöndro practice, and generally providing fellowship during the challenging work of the preliminary practices.
  4. We organized the security and venue team for Rabjam Rinpoche's 2023 visit to Boulder, helping to pass on the tradition of mindfully protecting visiting dignitaries and guests.
