Dharma Materials Project

View: Practitioners have accumulated dharma materials and need an appropriate way to pass them on. At the same time, newer practitioners need those materials and lack access to them.

Method: Receiving and passing on books, pratice texts, and ritual objects, respecting restrictions.

Completed Programs

  1. Seminary Transcript Drive (2024 – 2025: We received and joyfully delivered many boxes of Vajradhatu Seminary transcripts to participants in the 3 Yanas Retreat at Drala Mountain Center. We plan to do this again in 2026!
  2. Grief on the Path of Dharma (2024): a hybrid program on love, loss, and grief featuring Miriam Tarcov, Jon Barbieri, Bryan Welch, and Colin Stubbert, moderated by Judy Lief. It was attended by about 100 people.
  3. Abhisheka Preparation (2023-2024): We helped students prepare for the Vajrayogini Abhisheka given by Ringu Tulku in Halifax in 2024. FES arranged reading transmissions, ngöndro practice, and provided fellowship during the challenging work of the preliminaries.
  4. Rabjam Rinpoche Visit (2023): FES ran the security and venue team for Rabjam Rinpoche's 2023 visit to Boulder, in cooperation with Rimé Society and Naropa University. He bestowed empowerments for the Sadhana of Manahumdra, Gesar, Guyagarbha and Vajrakilaya. FES provided training for several volunteers and hosted around 600 participants.
